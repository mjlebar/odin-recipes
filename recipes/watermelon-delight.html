Watermelon Delight

Description

This recipe makes two delicious watermelon delights - a sweet, cold beverage that's as easy to make as it is fun to drink!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Blend watermelon, ice cubes, and honey in a blender until smooth.
